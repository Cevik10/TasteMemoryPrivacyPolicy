Privacy Policy for TasteMemory

Effective date: May 6, 2026

TasteMemory ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how TasteMemory handles information when you use our iOS application.

1) Summary

TasteMemory is designed as a private, on-device archive. Your entries, collections, notes, and media are stored locally on your device.

2) Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data on our own servers.

  • Account data: We do not require account creation.
  • User content: Your archive content is stored on-device.
  • Analytics: We do not run third-party analytics trackers in the app.

3) In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

TasteMemory offers optional premium subscriptions and/or lifetime purchases through Apple’s App Store.

  • Payments are processed by Apple, not by us.
  • We use RevenueCat to support subscription status and restore flows.
  • RevenueCat may process limited purchase-related identifiers (for example, anonymous app user IDs, transaction metadata, and subscription status) to provide these features.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

4) Photos and Media Access

If you choose to attach images to entries, TasteMemory may request access to your photo library. This is used only to let you select and save content in the app.

We do not upload your photos to our servers.

5) Data Storage and Security

  • Your archive data is stored locally on your device.
  • You can delete your content any time from inside the app.
  • When you uninstall the app, locally stored app data may be removed by iOS.

6) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share your archive content with advertisers.

Limited data may be shared with service providers only as needed for core functionality, such as purchases and subscription management (e.g., Apple and RevenueCat).

7) Children’s Privacy

TasteMemory is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) International Users

If third-party providers (such as Apple or RevenueCat) process data, they may do so in jurisdictions outside your country under their own policies and safeguards.

9) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding personal data. Since we do not maintain user accounts or a centralized archive database:

  • You can manage/delete your content directly on your device.
  • For purchase-related records, you may need to contact Apple or RevenueCat directly.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the effective date above.
